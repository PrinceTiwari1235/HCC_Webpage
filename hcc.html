A Legacy of Learning and Leadership

At HCC, we’re committed to delivering the highest standard of academic excellence while nurturing each student’s creativity, character, and confidence through a well-rounded, values-driven curriculum.

Hetauda City College (HCC) believes education is more than just academics — it’s about shaping well-rounded individuals who can confidently face the future. Established in 2065 B.S., our college has been a pillar of quality education in Hetauda, combining traditional values with innovative teaching methods. Our dedicated faculty focuses on fostering creativity, critical thinking, and strong character in every student. We provide a safe, nurturing environment where students are encouraged to explore their interests through a diverse range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and community engagement.
